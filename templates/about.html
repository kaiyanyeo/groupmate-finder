ABOUT

This is an online application to help people find suitable groupmates. Taking into account work abilities, work preferences and personalities, this application automatically helps find someone similar to you for you to group together.


With various functions to help match suitable people with you, we hope that you will have a much more enjoyable experience working with people of similar abilities and personalities.
